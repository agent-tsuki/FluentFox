<Concept title="What is を?">
を (written as wo, pronounced o) marks the direct object — the thing that the action is done to.
</Concept>

<Example japanese="りんごをたべます。" romaji="Ringo wo tabemasu." english="I eat an apple." concept="What is を?" />

<Example japanese="にほんごをべんきょうします。" romaji="Nihongo wo benkyou shimasu." english="I study Japanese." concept="What is を?" />
